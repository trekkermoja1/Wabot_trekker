How to pair your WhatsApp:

  1. Enter your bot name and phone number above
  2. Click "Get Pairing Code" to receive your code
  3. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  4. Go to Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device
  5. Choose "Link with phone number instead"
  6. Enter the pairing code shown
